how to approch the problem ? 

1. Understand the system - make sure you understand what the question system mean in the simplest sense. And talk it out to make sure you and other guy are in the same page. 
2. Requirment gathering : From the interviewer and oneself. Gather whatever we might be needed in the system. 
		also focus on the - 
		- Ask about **functional requirements** — what features must the system support? and the functional requirments you infered.
		- Ask about **non-functional requirements** — scale, concurrency, read/write ratio?
		- Confirm the **scope** — what's in and out of scope?
3. Core entities identification : 
		- Extract the **main objects** from the problem statement 
			- These are our core classes. 
4. Realtionship understanding between the entities.
	- Is it **inheritance** (IS-A)
	- Is it **composition** (HAS-A)
	- Is it **association**? (CAN-DO)
5. Class diagram and ER Diagram making.
